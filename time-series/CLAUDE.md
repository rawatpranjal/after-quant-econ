# Time Series Models

## Models in this section
Stochastic-process tutorials and empirical time series analysis of
macroeconomic data using FRED and Stock-Watson methods.

## Common patterns
- Data loading from FRED-MD dataset (CSV)
- Analytic AR process calculations, simulated paths, autocorrelation, spectra,
  and impulse responses
- Factor models and principal components
- Forecasting and impulse responses from reduced-form VARs

## Key economics
- Persistence as an economic timing assumption
- Macroeconomic monitoring with high-dimensional data
- Stock-Watson diffusion index methodology
- Business cycle measurement and dating
