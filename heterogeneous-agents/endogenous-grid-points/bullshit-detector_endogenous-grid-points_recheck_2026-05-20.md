### Finding 1 (original): Perfect-foresight MPC limit formula was wrong

- **Original verdict:** FALSE, MED. Formula was `R*(beta_r**(-1/gamma)) - 1` (= R/G_c - 1 = 0.0413), not the Carroll formula.
- **Fix applied:** `run.py:335` now reads `mpclim = 1.0 - (beta_r ** (1.0 / gamma)) / gross_return`.
- **Verification:** At beta_r=0.9785, gamma=2.0, gross_return=1.03: G_c = 0.9785^0.5 ≈ 0.98920, mpclim = 1 - 0.98920/1.03 ≈ 0.0396. README:158 and `tables/summary-statistics.csv:10` both read `0.0396`. README:140 prose reads `\kappa^{\ast}\approx0.040` and states the formula `\kappa^{\ast}=1-(\beta R)^{1/\gamma}/R`.
- **Status: RESOLVED.**
